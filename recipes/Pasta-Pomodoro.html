Pasta Pomodoro

Pasta Pomodoro is a simple tomato-based pasta.

Pasta Pomodoro is a traditional Italian dish made with pasta, fresh tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, and basil. It's light, flavorful, and easy to prepare, highlighting the freshness of its ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add canned tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the sauce.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.
